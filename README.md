## ðŸ“œ Our Code of Conduct
To maintain a high-performing and healthy engineering culture, we adhere to these core principles:

### 1. Radical Candor
We believe in giving and receiving direct, actionable feedback. Challenge ideas directly, but care about each other personally.

### 2. Bias for Action
In the face of ambiguity, we prefer a "v1" that works over a perfect "v0" that never ships. Document your decisions, but keep moving.

### 3. Ownership & Accountability
If you see a bug or a gap in documentation, you own the fix. We don't say "that's not my job."

### 4. Respectful Collaboration
* **Code Reviews:** Treat PRs as a teaching tool, not a gatekeeping mechanism. Be kind and constructive.
* **Inclusivity:** We are a global team. Respect different time zones, cultural backgrounds, and communication styles.
* **Disagreement:** It is okay to disagree. However, once a decision is made, we "disagree and commit" to move the project forward.
